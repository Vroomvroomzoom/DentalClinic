Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

At Bright Smile Dental, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our website.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from visitors to this website. We do not use contact forms, user accounts, or newsletters.

2. No Cookies or Tracking

This website does not use cookies, trackers, or analytics services (like Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel). We do not track your browsing behavior.

  • Instagram (operated by Meta Platforms, Inc.) — linked for our social media presence. Usage is governed by Meta's privacy policy.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them before interacting with these links. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party services.
